Our Approach

Preserving Tradition, Spreading Devotion

Since 1937, Durga Puja Samiti, Sarmera has been committed to nurturing devotion towards Maa Durga and preserving the cultural heritage of our land. Through grand celebrations, community participation, and cultural programs, we continue to pass on the values of faith, unity, and service to future generations.

  • Durga Puja
  • Temple
  • Pandit

Durga Puja is more than a festival for us — it is a time of devotion, joy, and togetherness. Each year, the Samiti organizes the celebration with traditional rituals, cultural performances, and community feasts, welcoming devotees from near and far.

Our temple stands as the spiritual heart of Sarmera, where daily prayers and special worship ceremonies connect devotees to the divine. It serves as a place of peace, devotion, and cultural unity for the entire community.

Our respected pandits guide the rituals with deep knowledge of Vedic traditions, ensuring that every puja is performed with sanctity and devotion. They also share spiritual wisdom with devotees, helping preserve our sacred customs.
